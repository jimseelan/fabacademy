Notes on the Final Project

Following are the notes on my project which I am putting together in the Applications and Implications week, and its also part of the assighnment for that week

What is the name of it?

WalkWorkWheel or the FabWheel

How will it look?

It will be like a giant hamster wheel, and instead of the hamsters we humans could use it.

what will it do?

We could walk on it, And Work while Waling on it. Also it has sensors that measures the rotations and a companion app that tracks your walks.

who's done what beforehand?

Giant Hamster wheels seems to have facinated humans for a long time now, and we could find images of many prototypes in the internet. (Ref Google, Bing).

And the most detailed one that I could find is this instructable, which depicts the creation of one very similar to the one being tried by me.

what materials and components will be required?

The wheel and the supporting structure could be made by plywood. Four small wheels, some threaded rods and fasteners will do with the mechanical part. Then on the electronics, will be needing a sensor (say a reed sensor) an a blutooth module. And a Android phone for the companion app.

where will they come from?

The plywood could be bought from Chalai Market(Ref this guide on Chalai). And the electronics sensors and bluetooth modules could be bought online from amazon and inkocean.in.

how much will it cost?

The initial estimate is 20,000INR of which 80% will be for the plywood and mechanical hardware.

what parts and systems will be made?

The entire wheel capable of accomadating a 6.5 foot tall person will be made, together with the electronics and the companion app interface.

what processes will be used?

Computer-aided design (NX, Autodesk Fusion)
Computer-controlled cutting and machining (Laser cutter and Shopbot)

3D printing (Ultimaker)

Electronics design and production (Eagle and Modella)
Eembedded programming (Arduino IDE)
Application interface (Android using App Inventor)

what tasks need to be completed?

Mechanical Parts
Design of the Mechanical parts
Scale model using Acryllic
Cut Full scale model on plywood
Assemble and test full scale model
Electronics and Software
Do a protype of functionality using Arduino
Design of a board to interface the sensor and bluetooth
Do the programming of the board
Finalize the app features, and create app.

what questions need to be answered?

What features should be there on the app?
Could I employ a more fun feedback than the app? Will I be able to finish all these as per plan?

what is the schedule?

Complete Mechanical tasks : 6-Jun
Complete Electronics and App : 8-Jun

how will it be evaluated?

This could be evaluated based on the process followed and the testing of the final prototype.
