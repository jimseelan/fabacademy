About

A photo of me in front of a mystery tree, on the Agastyarkoodam mountain

I am a Mechanical Engineer by graduation and also have a post graduate degree in Product Design and Development. I am working with the engineering services team of a large consulting firm, for a decade now. And in my current role I am mentoring fresh recruits during their initial training months at my organisation.

My heart was always on making, or more correctly, 'trying to make' things. Which has recently being boosted with the energy and curiosity inspired from my little son Ethan. I came to know about fablab via an news paper article, and as soon as I visited it about 2 years back, I had a feeling of being a child again having lot of toys to playwith. As part of the training project we have used the fablab a couple of times. And I did not had a second thought to join fabacademy when I learned about it this year. And after the go-ahead from my boss, here I am doing the fabacademy on part-time mode.

I currently reside at Trivandrum, in Southern India, with my wife Nithya and son Ethan. I am very excited to be part of Fabacademy 2017, and I hope to learn the many tools and techniques that could help me make (nearly) anyhing as the course tag-line goes.
